Welcome to the Task Generator application!

Here's how to use it:

1. Enter name of your task in the 'Name' field. Your name can only contain letters and numbers.

2. Select a setting from the 'Setting' dropdown menu.

3. Select a goal from the 'Goal' dropdown menu.

4. Click the 'Generate' button to generate a task based on your name, setting, and goal.

5. A new window will open displaying your generated task. You can close this window when you're done reading the task.

6. If you want to generate a new task, simply change the name, setting, or goal and click the 'Generate' button again.

Enjoy using the Task Generator!
